6 Reasons Why You'll Love OSCAL Tab 9

Hi guys,

Today is a very cool topic about 6 reasons to love buying the amazing OSCAL Tablet 9. Although I only experienced this tablet for less than half a month, it gets me quickly.

1.Sleek Design

I love its appearance at the first sight. This tablet gives a perfect fusion of style and comfort. It’s 8.8mm thin and very comfortable to hold. Made of aluminum, the back cover wraps the back of the tablet completely and blends seamlessly with the edges of the screen. The four sides are well curved to make it look more elegant.

2.10.1-inch Display

The 10.1-inch display with narrow bezels enlarges the field of vision. 1920*1200 FHD resolution shows clear and vivid details. So whether you are watching videos or gaming, you get the immersive viewing experience.

3.7480mAh Large-capacity Battery

This tablet really surprises me with the long battery life as few tablets can be equipped with such a high-capacity battery. It easily supports 6 hours of video, 6 hours of game and 7.5 hours of web browsing. I always use it to watch news on the way to work. And it takes several days for the next charging.

4.Excellent Octa-core Processor

OSCAL tab 9 is powered by an octa-core Spreadtrum T610 processor to improve the CPU and GPU performance by 20%. And thanks to this processor, It’s smooth to play games. I can start up several work & play apps and switch among them swiftly. Unlike my previous tablet, I don’t need to close some apps to smoothly run others.

5.Smart Doke_P 1.0 OS

I am very pleased with the clean and clear user interface. The system manager provides a smart control over trash cleaning, battery performance, gaming boosting, and how apps run in the background so I can operate the tablet easier.

6.Friendly Pricing

This Tab 9 is available at a stunningly low price. I got this tablet at $149.99 (original price $199.99) during its promotion period. This price is fairly lower than the Apple, samsung and Huawei tablets but I can enjoy the comparable performance with these brands.
